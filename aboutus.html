Your Partner in Precision Flow Control

A leading industrial valve manufacturer in Mumbai, India, delivering trusted solutions since 2001.

For over two decades, Usmani Tubes & Valves Co. Pvt. Ltd. has been a cornerstone of the industrial valve manufacturing industry. Since our establishment in 2001, we have built a legacy of trust and quality, becoming a leading manufacturer, exporter, and supplier of high-performance valves.

As an ISO 9001:2008 certified company, our commitment to excellence is unwavering. We specialize in a comprehensive range of industrial valves, including Gate Valves, Globe Valves, Ball Valves, and Check Valves, all marketed under our trusted "UTVC" brand. Our products are engineered for reliability and serve critical functions across the globe in sectors like petrochemicals, power generation, and oil and gas. At Usmani Valves, we combine decades of experience with a dedication to quality to deliver products you can depend on.

What Drives Us

Our mission is to lead the industrial valve market by manufacturing and supplying superior-quality valves that enhance the reliability and efficiency of our clients' operations. We are dedicated to continuous innovation, competitive pricing, and on-time delivery to provide exceptional value and exceed customer expectations at every stage.

Insights & Innovations in Industrial Valves

A leading industrial valve manufacturer in Mumbai, India, delivering trusted solutions since 2001.

Reliable Flow Control for the Oil & Gas Industry

The Oil & Gas industry needs valves that work safely under extreme pressure and temperature. Usmani Valves offers durable Gate Valves, Ball Valves, and Control Valves designed for offshore and onshore challenges. Made from corrosion-resistant alloys, our valves ensure leak-proof performance and long service life. With advanced safety features like emergency shut-off, Usmani Valves helps refineries and pipelines prevent accidents and protect the environment. Trusted globally, our solutions deliver the perfect balance of safety, strength, and efficiency.

Engineered Valve Solutions for Petrochemical & Chemical Applications

In the chemical industry, even small leaks can cause big risks. Usmani Valves provides chemical-resistant Butterfly Valves, Diaphragm Valves, and Lined Valves for safe and reliable flow control. Built with PTFE linings and high-grade materials, our valves resist corrosion from acids, alkalis, and solvents. Precision engineering ensures smooth operations, reduces downtime, and supports compliance with safety standards. From production lines to storage plants, Usmani Valves delivers reliable, leak-proof, and efficient solutions that keep chemical processes safe.

Hygienic and Precise Valve Solutions for the Pharmaceutical Industry

Pharmaceutical manufacturing demands hygienic and contamination-free processes. Usmani Valves designs sanitary Diaphragm Valves, Butterfly Valves, and Stainless-Steel Valves that meet FDA and GMP standards. With smooth, polished surfaces and CIP/SIP compatibility, our valves are easy to clean and maintain. They provide accurate flow control, ensuring consistency in drug formulations and patient safety. By combining reliability with global compliance, Usmani Valves is the trusted partner for pharma companies seeking clean, safe, and precise valve solutions.
